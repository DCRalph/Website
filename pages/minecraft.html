Brocraft mod setup

Fabric Mod Loader

To play Brocraftmc you will need some mods. The mod loader is the we use is Fabric.
Downloads: Fabric

Immersive Portals

Immersive Portals is required as it modifys the fundamentals of portals.
Downloads: Immersive Portals

Roughly Enough Items

Roughly Enough Items is not required to play but will make crafting and finding recipes easier.
Downloads: Roughly Enough Items

Shulker Box Tooltip

Shulker Box Tooltip is not required to play. This mod enables you to have a look inside shulker boxes and ender chast without placing them. This mod comes in handy when searching for a specific item.
Downloads: Shulker Box Tooltip

Optfine

Optifabric is not required to play. This is a mod for fabric that adds the ability to use optfine. You will need the mod and the optfine jar. To install, put the optifabric.jar inside the mods folder as well as the optifine.jar
Downloads: Optifabric Optifine

Please use the downloads provided because the mods must be the right virsion.
